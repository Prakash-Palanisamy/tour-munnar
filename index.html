Our affordable services

  • Trekking

    LETCHMI HILLS RIDGE: Trek through the luscious tea plantations and verdant grasslands to get a birds' eye view of misty Munnar.
    TOP VALLEY Kerala-Tamilnadu border with thick shola patches and Wildlife
    MEESAPULIMALAI Highest trek in South India through rolling grass lands

  • Tea Experiences

    DAY WITH TEA PICKER: On reaching, you will follow the tea trail to the area where the ladies will be picking tea leaves. Once in the area, you will join them in picking tea leaves and in weighing the same, all the while interacting with the workers to understand their way of life. Finally, you get to visit the home of one of the pickers and have a cup of tea before returning

  • Wildlife/Herping tours

    PAMBADUM SHOLA NATIONAL PARK
    This national park offers the best opportunity to spot the Nilgiri marten and is also known for Indian gaur, sambar deer, Nilgiri langur, Malabar giant squirrel and possibly, elephants.
    CHINNAR WILDLIFE SANCTUARY
    Animal enthusiasts have the opportunity of potting tufted grey langur, slender loris, elephants, gaur, crocodiles and on a lucky day, leopards.

  • Mountain Cycling

    HALF DAY CYCLING: In this acticity you will enter the relatively flat and calmness of Letchmi road leading to up to the estate. Letchmi estate is one of the most picturesque tea plantations in Munnar and the landscape as you continue to cycle down the road is a pure visually treat. As you pass through the village, you can see domestic animals on the sides of the road, tiny homes belonging to plantation workers arranged in neat, meticulous lines and vibrantly coloured temples.

  • Munnar history walk

    We will embark on a 03 hours STORY TELLING session on Munnar coupled with visits to some of the iconic landmarks in around Munnar which stands testament to the passing of history. The tour includes Head Works Dam (used for first hydroelectricity project in Kerala), CSI, RC churches built during colonial times, second World war memorial, Ropeway station (which is the present logistic office for KDHP), Lodge Heather etc. The walk will also give you a glimpse of present day Munnar and its customs and is the perfect opportunity to get to know your holiday spot better.

  • South Indian cuisine

    Mountain Cycling

    MAKE YOUR OWN MEAL (MUNNAR STYLE) WITH A HOME MAKER Includes shopping for ingredients in the market followed by cooking demonstration followed by dinner. The shopping experience will help the guest to learn what items are required for cooking the specific dishes.

  • 17C Camping Lockhart

    This scenic campsite is situated in the outskirts of Munnar in 1600acre tea plantation sprawls in the foothills of Chokkarmudi. Lockhart is one of the oldest tea estates in Munnar.

  • Camp Footprint - Glamping

    Camp Footprint consists of three comfortable stays set amidst the native shola forest to give you the perfect taste of wilderness. At Camp Footprint you get a cosy bed, private en-suite facilities, great local food, personalized service and an unbeatable view right from your room.
